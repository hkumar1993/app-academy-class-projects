Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • flour
  • eggs
  • bread crumbs
  • seasoning
  • sunflower oil

Directions

  1. preheat oil to 425° F
  2. break and whisk egg in bowl
  3. place flour and bread crumbs in seperate dishes
  4. coat chicken with flour
  5. dip floured chicked in egg
  6. coat chicken withn break crumbs
  7. fry chicken in oil for 10 mins or until skin is brown
  8. just eat it
